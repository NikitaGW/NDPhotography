About Me

Capturing moments, telling stories, creating art

Photographer's Bio and Story

Hello, I'm Sarah Johnson, a professional photographer with over 12 years of experience capturing life's most precious moments. My journey began when I received my first camera as a birthday gift at age 14, and I've been obsessed with photography ever since.

After studying Fine Arts at the University of Washington, I worked as an assistant to several renowned photographers, which helped me develop my unique style. In 2015, I decided to start my own photography business, and I haven't looked back since.

What drives me is the ability to freeze time - to capture emotions, relationships, and beauty that might otherwise be forgotten. Whether it's a wedding, a portrait session, or a commercial project, I approach each assignment with the same passion and attention to detail.

My Photography Philosophy

See the Unseen

I believe in looking beyond the obvious to capture the subtle emotions and details that tell the real story.

Authenticity Matters

My goal is to create images that feel genuine, not staged. I guide rather than pose, allowing natural moments to unfold.

Light is Everything

I work with light to create mood and depth, whether it's soft natural light or carefully crafted artificial lighting.
