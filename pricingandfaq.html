FAQs About Pricing & Booking

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, bank transfers, UPI payments, and cash. A 25% deposit is required to secure your booking, with the balance due on the day of your session.

How long does it take to receive our photos?

Our standard delivery time is 2-3 weeks for portrait sessions and 4-6 weeks for weddings. You'll receive a preview selection within 3-5 days after your session.

What is your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable but can be applied to a rescheduled session within 6 months. Cancellations within 14 days of the session date require 50% of the total package price.

Do you offer discounts for weekday bookings?

Yes! We offer 15% off for sessions booked Monday through Thursday. This discount doesn't apply to weddings or holiday periods.

Can we request specific shots or poses?

Absolutely! We encourage clients to share inspiration photos or a shot list before the session. We'll incorporate your ideas while also providing our professional guidance.
